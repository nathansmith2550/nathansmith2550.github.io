The Coach

Shane Valko

NCAA Hall of Fame

Shane Valko founded Silent Victory Training Center in 2013 to give athletes of every level a home for wrestling, Jiu-Jitsu, and strength training. His path started on the wrestling mat and never really left it — a competitive career that turned into a coaching career built on the same discipline he demands in the room today.

After a decorated collegiate run, Shane moved into professional MMA and now competes as an undefeated professional Jiu-Jitsu brown belt. His coaching résumé speaks for itself: dozens of All-Americans and national champions have come through his program.

"Every challenge is an opportunity to grow stronger, and every victory is earned through the effort and commitment in the solitude of silence."
